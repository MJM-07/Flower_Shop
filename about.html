🌸 About Us

The story behind every bloom we deliver

Our Story

Bloom & Petal was born in 2018 from a simple love of flowers. What started as a small corner stall in Dhaka has grown into a beloved flower shop trusted by thousands of customers across the city.

Every morning, our team hand-selects the freshest blooms from local growers so that every bouquet we create is full of life, fragrance, and colour. We believe flowers are more than decoration — they carry emotions, memories, and love.

Whether it's a single rose or a grand wedding arrangement, we pour our hearts into every petal.
